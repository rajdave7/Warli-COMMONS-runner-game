Warli Runner Game

About the Game

Welcome to the Warli Runner Game! In this game, you play as a member of the Warli community, running through their beautiful landscapes filled with natural resources such as trees, ponds, and mountains. As you encounter these resources, you'll witness how the Warli people respect and utilize them as shared resources, not commodities for profit.

Throughout your journey, you'll also encounter fellow community members. After reaching certain milestones and encountering natural resources, the community members will come together to perform the Tarpa dance around a fire, symbolizing the values of brotherhood and co-dependence.

The game aims to spread awareness about the concept of "Commons" and how the Warli community views nature and natural resources as shared assets for societal benefit and survival. By immersing yourself in the Warli culture and their harmonious relationship with nature, you'll gain a deeper appreciation for the importance of preserving our environment.

Game Features

  • Immersive Warli art-themed graphics
  • Runner gameplay with obstacles and milestones
  • Showcases Warli community values and traditions
  • Integration of Warli Tarpa music
  • Educational content about the concept of "Commons"
